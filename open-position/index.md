## Current Openings

### Postdoctoral Scholar

We are seeking a postdoctoral scholar with expertise in single-cell genomics, computational biology, or tumor immunology to join our team at The Ohio State University Comprehensive Cancer Center.

**Qualifications:**
- PhD in Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, Cancer Biology, or related field
- Experience with single-cell RNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, or multi-omics data analysis
- Strong programming skills in Python and/or R
- Publication record in peer-reviewed journals

### Graduate Students

We welcome rotation students and prospective PhD students interested in computational oncology, multi-omics integration, and translational cancer research.
